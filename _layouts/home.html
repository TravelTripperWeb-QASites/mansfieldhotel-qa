Welcome to
The Mansfield Hotel

The historic landmark 126-room Mansfield Hotel enjoys a prime location in the heart of the city on 44th Street between Fifth and Sixth Avenues. Broadway theatres, fine restaurants, shopping, and celebrated sites are all within a short walking distance.
