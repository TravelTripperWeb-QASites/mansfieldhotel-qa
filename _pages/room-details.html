The jennings deluxe suite

The Mansfield's Deluxe Suite - The Jennings Apartment is approx. 420 square feet and features customized etched-glass French doors designed by Denise Amses of Amses Coma, a decorative fireplace, ebony-stained hardwood floors, and a separate parlor area. The suite includes one king-sized bed, which is complemented by luxurious linens, down comforter and pillow-topped mattress along with a spacious separate living area. The Deluxe Suite-The Jennings Apartment is appointed with customized robes, iHome Alarm Clock Radio for your iPod, two Dell Flat Panel LCD TVs, mini bar and Philosophy bath products. Perfect for the business traveler, this room is equipped with a writing desk and high-speed wireless internet access.
